Why become a member?

Create legislation

Create legislation that beyond regulation, will promote innovation and business opportunities.

Health informatics research

Conduct cutting edge health informatics research generating and translating evidence into knowledge and applying that knowledge in continuously in solving our health needs.

Be part of the front line

You get to take part in the effort to seize and utilize the utility value of comprehensive and appropriate implementation of Health Information Technology (HIT) in Kenya.

Take part in forums through working groups of your interest which could support initiatives to improve and sustain high quality and effective health services for all Kenyans.

Networks

Create networks with professionals in the field of health and health informatics through the online membership directory. Gain social and professional networks through working groups for collaborations, advisors and colleagues.

Discounts

Get access to health and health informatics journals through the association at discounted rates. Discounted rates for meetings and conferenece organized, sponsored or co-sponsored by KeHIA. Corporates can market their products through the association website and journal at discounted rates.
